Why Notes matter most: a Notes follow auto-adds the person to the publication's email list, so growing Note reach and growing the paid funnel are the same action. Treat Notes like 2020 Twitter and farm them daily. Pair with the Voice skill, Reply Protocol, and Adam Knowledge System Ops.

## The 6-point Note machine
1. Cold open like a text to a friend: a concrete scene with one small wrongness. Sensory, dollar, or time detail creates credibility.
2. One sentence per line. White space is pacing. Phone-readable beats smooth AI prose.
3. Name the thing: `Acts 8:37`, `Perry Stone`, `17 years`, never vague placeholders.
4. Front-load the verdict: hardest claim in line 1, defend it backward.
5. One open loop OR one CTA. Never stack asks.
6. Any link belongs in a self-reply after approval, never the note body.

## Hook menu
Paradigm Flip, Objective Authority, Mirror Test, Velvet Rope, Action-Talk Gap, open with a number, scene plus loop plus twist, or a reply-chain prompt.

## Niche split
- The Biblical Man = the sword: KJV/translation discernment, biblical manhood, the household, repentance.
- Dead Hidden = the scar: forgotten or uncomfortable passages, spiritual warfare, the household under attack. Reverent and investigative, never conspiracy overstatement.

## What kills a Note
Vague `you`, rage with no defensible verse, soft openers, two asks, more than one note per 3 to 4 hours per stack, and links in the body. Judge free Notes by follows/clicks to the offer, not raw views.
