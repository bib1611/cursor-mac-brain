# LOSERS — what died, so the brain stops repeating it

Half of getting smarter is knowing what to never do again. The model reads this after `winners.md` and treats every entry as a "do not run this angle/topic/format again without a reason." A loser is not a failure. It is data you paid for. Log it once and stop paying twice.

**What counts as a loser:** a post that died under ~300 impressions or ~20 likes in 24 hours despite real effort, OR a confrontation that drew blocks/mutes/reports without tribe-rally (Tweepcred damage), OR a sales push that got impressions but zero conversions.

## The kill-list (distilled — read this before every draft)

The standing "never again" / "watch this" rules earned from real data.
- Naked confrontation with no defensible seam. (PART 2 Confrontation Governor.)
- Vague "you" with no specific observable behavior.
- Links in the post body. Suppressed reach. Link goes in the self-reply.
- Two SKUs in one post.
- Posting more than ~once per 3-4 hours.
- **FREE Substack posts underperform PAID on views.** Real gap: BM/DH paid posts cluster 9k-11k views, the lowest mature performers are often the `everyone`/`only_free` posts. The free post is the top-of-funnel hook, so its job is the CLICK and the upgrade prompt, not the view count. Do not judge a free post by views — judge it by clicks to the offer.
- **High views + near-zero comments = reach without a reply loop.** Several 9k-10k DH posts pulled under 5 comments. They reached but did not engage. If a post is built for reach, fine; if it was meant to start a conversation, it failed. Build the question in.
- **Declarative ALL-CAPS prophetic posts reach but rarely convert clicks.** "THEY CALL IT ALIEN. GOD CALLED IT FALLEN." hit 10k views but the discernment-Bible hooks ("Most Christians do not need a new Bible") out-clicked it 2:1 at lower views. For a CONVERSION post, use the Bible-discernment angle, not the cosmic-spectacle angle.
