## Core thesis
Growth is rarely one viral moment. It is consistency, systems, relationships, repetition, iteration, daily reps, until momentum compounds. You do not rise to your motivation, you fall to your systems. Most accounts look stagnant a long time, then accelerate. The ones who win simply stay around longer.

## Daily non-negotiables
Publish daily. 50+ high-quality replies. Save 5 content ideas. Study winning posts. Repurpose old content. Engage right after publishing. Schedule future posts.

## Writing habit
Start ridiculously small. Never miss twice. Draft first, edit later. Consistency over perfection. Identity over motivation. Remove friction: open the doc before you sit, keep notes and assets organized.

## Content pillars
Sit at the intersection of curiosity, knowledge, experience, and audience interest. Broad enough for flexibility, specific enough for identity. "Travel" is weak; "hidden towns / forgotten places / cultural history" is strong. Specificity creates recognition.

## Reply strategy (the early engine)
Insert yourself into conversations that already have attention. Great replies stand alone, add an angle, create emotion, read fast, lean slightly contrarian, feel like mini posts. Weak: "Beautiful place." Strong: "Places like this remind you how much of the world still hasn't been optimized." Speed beats perfection.

## Escalation / repurposing
One strong idea becomes post, thread, article, newsletter, video, carousel, reply, note. Rewrite a strong idea at least 6 ways. Same idea performs differently by wording, visual, timing, format, emotional framing. Never assume it failed after one try.

## Hook frameworks (full patterns in swipe-files/hooks.md)
Contrarian, Secret Observation, Hidden Reality, Extreme Rarity, Mistake (loss aversion), Process. Winning hooks include curiosity, specificity, emotion, tension, contrast, numbers, strong visual.

## Research framework
Most viral ideas already exist in fragments. Mine Reddit, YouTube, comments, X bookmarks, Grok, Perplexity. Comments are often more valuable than the post. Look for emotion, recurring frustration, controversy, hidden gems.

## Credibility loop and specificity
Layer proof on proof: examples, statistics, quotes, specifics, imagery, named references. Vague content is forgettable, specific content feels believable.

## Distribution > creation
Publishing is the beginning of distribution. Reply, engage immediately, leverage communities, reshare evergreen winners, keep content circulating.

## AI usage
Best for brainstorming, research, organization, repurposing, spotting blind spots, building systems. Pair with human taste. It accelerates execution, it does not replace originality.
