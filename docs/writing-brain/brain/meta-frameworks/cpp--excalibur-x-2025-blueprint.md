# Excalibur X 2025 — Yearly Blueprint

## Source
- File: `EXCALIBUR_X_2025.txt`
- Type: Excalibur "Your Blueprint for 2025" — yearly roadmap worksheet (10 action items)
- Teacher / program: Create Publish Profit (CPP), createpublishprofit.com
- Topic: A sequenced 10-step year plan for building, monetizing, and scaling an X account.

## Core thesis
Growth on X is a fixed order of operations, not a pile of tactics. You qualify the account, organize your inputs, define purpose, build relationships, get paid, hype, build the list, create offers, set yearly targets — in that sequence. Each step unlocks the next.

## Key frameworks / rules
The 10 Action Items, in order:

1. **Qualify** — Become verified. Enable two-factor ID. Avoid all forms of spam. Avoid posting links. Increase TweetCred score.
2. **Organize** — Find mentor accounts. Subscribe to mentors. Build a library of content. Create lists (and follow them). Write everything down. Develop a notes system.
3. **Define Purpose** — Write an account-definition post. Pin it / highlight it on the profile.
4. **Build Relationships** — Subscribe and turn on notifications. 25 natural comments per day. Turn comments into content. Post every single day. Attend and speak at Spaces. DM one new person per day. Start making friends.
5. **Get Paid** — Get over 500 Premium followers. 5 million impressions in 3 months. Qualify for ad revenue sharing.
6. **Hype** — Host weekly Spaces. Threads twice a week. Share in communities. Support in communities. Build networking groups.
7. **Build** — Build an email list. Create a landing page. Create a lead magnet. Get over 2,000 Premium followers. Apply for subscriptions.
8. **Create** — Create a low-ticket offer. Sell to your audience. Make money. Listen and apply feedback. Get testimonials. Create a high-ticket offer.
9. **Define Yearly Goals** — Follower target. Revenue target. Email subscriber target. Life goals plus biz goals.
10. **Apply for Mentorship** — Apply at createpublishprofit.com.

Operating rules pulled from the worksheet:
- **No links in posts.** Link-posting is named under "Qualify" as a thing to avoid — protect reach first.
- **Comments are the engine.** 25 natural comments per day, plus DM one new person per day. Relationships come before money.
- **Comments into content.** Mine your own replies for post material; nothing said once is wasted.
- **Post every single day.** Daily cadence is non-negotiable, listed under relationship-building, not under "content."
- **Verification and TweetCred gate everything.** You qualify the account before you try to sell from it.
- **List the people you study.** Find mentors, subscribe, build a content library and a notes system before you create.

## Hook / thread / offer patterns
The worksheet names the structure, not the wording. Brand-adapted patterns for The Biblical Man (KJV discernment) and Follow Me (scar-tissue):

- **Account-definition post (Action Item 3).** One pinned post that states who you are and who you serve. Biblical Man version: open with the line in the sand, not a bio. "I teach men to read the King James and test every spirit. No NIV. No soft gospel. Pinned so you know where you stand." Follow Me version: lead with the scar. "Garbage truck driver. Train conductor. Five kids. Saved late, and I write from the wound."
- **Threads twice a week (Action Item 6).** Long-form proof, not a daily habit. Use threads for the deep discernment teach-down; use daily posts for the punch.
- **Offer ladder (Action Items 7-8).** Lead magnet, then low-ticket offer, then high-ticket offer, in that order. Test the low-ticket, gather testimonials, then build the high-ticket. Do not sell the high-ticket cold.
- **Self-reply for links.** Since links in the post body suppress reach, put the offer or Substack link in the first self-reply.

## Numbers and proof
- 25 natural comments per day.
- 1 new-person DM per day.
- Threads twice a week.
- Over 500 Premium followers — the "Get Paid" gate.
- 5 million impressions in 3 months — ad revenue sharing qualifier.
- Over 2,000 Premium followers — the "Build" / subscriptions tier.
- CPP group mentorship described as month-long, beginning January 9th, with group calls, video lessons, and live ghostwriting sessions, all recorded.

## Brand application (Adam's voice)
This is a checklist, not a craft lesson. Most of it Adam already runs. What still earns its place:

- Daily posting plus 25 real comments a day is the relationship engine. Not broadcast. Reply where the men already gather.
- Threads twice a week, not daily. Save the long teach-down for the threads. Keep the daily posts short and sharp.
- One pinned account-definition post. State the wall: KJV only, test every spirit, write from the wound. Let the reader self-select.
- Run the offer ladder in order. Lead magnet, low-ticket, testimonials, then high-ticket. Proof before price.
- Never link in the body. Self-reply with the open.substack.com link, r=2t2o3r.

What to ignore: TweetCred chasing, verification mechanics, Spaces hype, and the CPP upsell. Infra, not content.
