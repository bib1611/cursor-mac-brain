# Trademark Research Report: "Brick" & "Bible Brick" for Christian Screen-Locking/Focus App

**Research Date:** July 2025
**Purpose:** Evaluate trademark viability of "Brick" and "Bible Brick" and propose 10 alternative names for a Christian screen-locking/focus app targeting homeschool families.

---

## 1. EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

### Primary Recommendation: DO NOT USE "Bible Brick" — High Risk, Despite No Direct Registration

**"Bible Brick"** appears to have no existing USPTO trademark registration and no app by that name exists. However, **Brick LLC** (getbrick.app / getbrick.com), a fast-growing startup founded in 2023 by TJ Driver and Zach Nasgowitz, has established **common law trademark rights** to "Brick" in the app-blocking / screen-time space. They explicitly claim trademark ownership in their Terms of Service and have significant market presence (35,000+ reviews, coverage in Vogue, Business Insider, Washington Post, Consumer Reports). Using "Bible Brick" for a screen-locking app creates a **strong likelihood of confusion** with their brand in the same market segment (app-blocking/screen-time management).

**Key Risk Factors:**
- Brick LLC operates in the **exact same functional market** (app blocking / screen-time management)
- They have been in commerce since 2023 with substantial press and user base
- "Bible Brick" incorporates their entire mark and merely adds a descriptive term
- Even without federal registration, their common law rights could support a successful infringement claim

### Secondary Finding: "Bible Brick" is USPTO-Clear, But That's Not Enough
While no USPTO registration blocks "Bible Brick," trademark rights in the US arise from **use in commerce**, not registration. Brick LLC's established use gives them enforceable rights.

### Alternative Recommendation: Use one of the 10 researched alternatives below. The top picks are:
1. **FaithWall** (#1 pick — evocative, clear, strong domain potential)
2. **BibleShield** (#2 pick — clear at USPTO, strong Christian positioning)
3. **Cornerstone Lock** (#3 pick — rich biblical metaphor, but watch Cornerstone OnDemand in HR software)

## 4. RISK ASSESSMENT FOR "BIBLE BRICK"

### 4.1 Likelihood of Confusion Analysis

| Factor | Assessment | Risk |
|--------|------------|------|
| **Similarity of Marks** | "Bible Brick" contains the ENTIRETY of "Brick" — the dominant portion of both marks is identical | HIGH |
| **Similarity of Goods** | Both are app-blocking/screen-time management tools for mobile devices | HIGH |
| **Channels of Trade** | Both would be sold through App Store, Google Play, and Christian/family-focused marketing | HIGH |
| **Consumer Sophistication** | Consumers are general public (including teens, parents) — low sophistication in distinguishing similar app names | MEDIUM |
| **Strength of Prior Mark** | "Brick" is somewhat descriptive of a solid/blocking concept but has acquired distinctiveness through use | MEDIUM |
| **Actual Confusion Evidence** | None yet, but Brick LLC could argue dilution/tarnishment of their brand | LOW-MEDIUM |
| **Defendant's Intent** | Adding "Bible" suggests good-faith differentiation, but similarity remains problematic | MEDIUM |

### 4.2 Different Market/Different Goods Argument

**Arguments FOR differentiation:**
- Brick LLC sells a **physical NFC device** ($59 hardware) + free app; "Bible Brick" would be purely software
- "Bible Brick" targets **Christian homeschool families** specifically; Brick LLC targets general productivity/focus market
- "Bible Brick" includes Bible engagement/Scripture features that Brick LLC does not have
- The addition of "Bible" creates a different commercial impression (religious vs. secular)

**Arguments AGAINST differentiation:**
- Both products perform the **same core function**: blocking distracting apps on smartphones
- Both operate through a **mobile app** using Screen Time / Digital Wellbeing APIs
- The **channels of distribution overlap** (App Store, social media marketing to parents concerned about screen time)
- Brick LLC already markets to families: their website features testimonials from parents about "family phone addiction"
- "Bible Brick" could be seen as a **line extension** of Brick (e.g., "Brick for Christians")

### 4.3 Overall Risk Rating: **MODERATE TO HIGH**

While there are legitimate arguments that the marks serve different sub-markets, the core functional overlap is substantial. If Brick LLC files for federal registration (which they may be doing) and discovers "Bible Brick" in the market, they would have a credible claim for trademark infringement or at minimum a strong cease-and-desist position.

**The risk is not worth taking** given the availability of strong alternative names (see Section 5).

## 6. FINAL RECOMMENDATION

### Do NOT Use "Bible Brick"

The risk of a trademark conflict with Brick LLC, combined with the availability of superior alternatives, makes "Bible Brick" an unwise choice. Brick LLC is an active, well-funded, fast-growing company with media presence and explicit trademark claims. Even if you might technically defend a "Bible Brick" filing, the legal uncertainty and potential rebrand costs are not worth it.

### Top 3 Recommendations:

| Rank | Name | Confidence | Rationale |
|------|------|------------|-----------|
| **1** | **FaithWall** | Highest | Clearest trademark path, strongest emotional resonance, best domain availability, most distinctive |
| **2** | **BibleShield** | High | Strong Christian positioning, clear USPTO path, active/protective imagery |
| **3** | **StoneGuard** | High | Most distinctive as a single compound word, strong masculine imagery, no USPTO conflicts |

### Next Steps:
1. **Run a comprehensive trademark clearance search** through a trademark attorney for your top 2-3 picks
2. **Check exact domain availability** for .com, .app, and .org
3. **File an intent-to-use trademark application** as soon as a name is selected (USPTO filing fee ~$250-350/class)
4. **Consider filing in both Class 9** (downloadable software) **and Class 42** (software as a service)
5. **Monitor Brick LLC's trademark activity** — they may file for federal registration soon
