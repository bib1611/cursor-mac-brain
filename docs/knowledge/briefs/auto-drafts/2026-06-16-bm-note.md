# BM Substack Note — 2026-06-16

You lost the job you wanted.

You called it bad luck. You called it warfare. You called it the devil.

It was the angel.

Balaam beat the donkey three times. She was the only thing keeping him alive. The wall against his foot was not bad luck. It was a sword he could not see.

The closed door is not your enemy.

It is your covering.

Grace looks like obstruction when you are inside it. It only looks like mercy from the far side of the road.

If you have been hit three times in the same place, stop swinging at the donkey. Look up.
