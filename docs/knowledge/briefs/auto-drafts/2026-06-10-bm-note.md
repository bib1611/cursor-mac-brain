# BM Substack Note — 2026-06-10

You think you would have spotted the cult.

The robes. The compound. The smile that never blinks.

You would have walked out. You are sure.

The cult you cannot leave is not down a dirt road in Waco.

It is the one you built around yourself.

You are the prophet. You are the elder. You are the lone member.

And you defend it like your life depends on it.

Because in a way, it does.

The leader looks like Christ. He talks like Christ.

He just never tells you no.

That is the counterfeit. Yours.

The way out is one verse.

"I am crucified with Christ: nevertheless I live; yet not I, but Christ liveth in me." — Galatians 2:20

Not I.

The founder dies. The lone member dies. The security guard dies.

Then a real man walks out.
