read it again. slower.

"she gave also unto her husband with her; and he did eat."

with her. he was standing there the whole time.

we got told a story about a weak wife and a clever snake. the man shows up late and gets tricked. burn that cartoon.

he was there for the conversation. there when the serpent ran its mouth in his own garden. and the first man said nothing. no rebuke. no warning. silence was easier than war.

then he ate. and the world broke.

the first sin wasn't a bite. it was a man who would not speak.

the serpent still talks. he just changed venues. and most of us are still furniture in our own homes.

"quit you like men, be strong." open your mouth. the garden is already crawling.
