# they will mark you

They will call you weird first.

Then strange. Then dangerous.

That is the order. It does not run backward.

You prayed over your food where they could see it. You turned off the show the whole office quotes. The joke landed and your mouth stayed shut.

They noticed.

"Strait is the gate, and narrow is the way, which leadeth unto life, and few there be that find it."

Few. Read it again. Not most. Few.

The wide road is full because it costs nothing. No gate. No wall. Nobody checks your hands at the door. You can carry anything down it. Most do.

The narrow one has a gate. A gate means a wall. A wall means a side. And a side means there are people on the other one.

You are about to find out who.

The world loved you when you blurred. When you laughed at everything. When nothing you believed cost anyone a thing.

Start standing still and watch the temperature drop.

They hated Him before they hated you. Same crowd. Same reasons. He would not bend, so they built Him a cross.

You will not get a cross. You will get the smaller version. A colder table. A group chat that goes quiet. A name said with an edge.

Good.

If the whole room still likes you, you have not started walking yet.

The gate was built narrow on purpose. Wide enough for the marked. Too tight for the crowd.

Let them call you weird.

Wear it like a coat in the cold.

You are not lost.

You are early.
