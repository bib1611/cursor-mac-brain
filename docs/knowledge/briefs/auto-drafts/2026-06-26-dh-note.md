they will call you weird first.

then strange. then dangerous. that is the order. it does not run backward.

you prayed over your food where they could see it. you turned off the show the whole office quotes. the joke landed and your mouth stayed shut. they noticed.

"strait is the gate, and narrow is the way, which leadeth unto life, and few there be that find it."

few. read it again. not most. few.

the wide road is full because it costs nothing. the narrow one has a gate. a gate means a wall. a wall means a side. you are about to find out who is on the other one.

they hated Him before they hated you. same crowd. same reasons. He would not bend, so they built Him a cross.

if the whole room still likes you, you have not started walking yet.

let them call you weird. you are not lost. you are early.
