# BM Substack Note — 2026-06-19

The sin was never the first thing.

The lie was.

You did the thing because you believed the thing.

You believed the thing because somebody whispered it before you knew how to read.

Genesis 3. The serpent did not hand her the fruit. He handed her a sentence.

"Yea, hath God said."

The mouth got busy with the fruit only after the ear had already swallowed the lie.

You did not look at the screen because you wanted the screen. You looked at the screen because a sentence had been running in you for years that said you were alone and nobody was coming.

The sin is the fruit. The lie is the root.

Stop cutting at the fruit.
