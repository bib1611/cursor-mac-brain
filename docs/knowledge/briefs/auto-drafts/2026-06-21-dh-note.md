# DH Note

They put wings on a baby and called it heaven.

Pink cheeks. A little harp. A chubby cherub on a greeting card.

That is not what walked out of the pages.

Daniel was a prophet who stared down lions. One angel came near and his face went grey and his knees gave out. "There remained no strength in me." Daniel 10:8.

The trained Romans at the tomb shook and became as dead men.

Count how many times an angel opens with the same three words. Fear not. You do not say that to a man looking at a cute baby with wings. You say it to a man certain he is about to be unmade.

They shrank the messenger so the message would feel small.

But the mountain around Elisha is still full of horses and chariots of fire.

They were always there. You just got handed a cartoon and told to stop looking.
