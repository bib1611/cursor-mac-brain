# And Also After That

You were taught the giants died in the flood.

You were taught wrong.

Read the verse.

Genesis 6:4.

"There were giants in the earth in those days; and also after that, when the sons of God came in unto the daughters of men, and they bare children to them, the same became mighty men which were of old, men of renown."

Stop on the phrase.

"And also after that."

The flood killed eight survivors into a clean world.

Then it happened again.

## What Most Preachers Skip

The Sunday School flannelgraph stops at the rainbow.

Not metaphorically.

Not as a literary trope.

In the earth. With the daughters of men. Mighty men of renown.

After.

That word is the whole map.

## What "After" Means for the Rest of the Bible

Joshua walked into Canaan and found the Anakim.

The Anakim were giants.

David killed Goliath in a valley because Goliath's father had four more brothers behind him and they were not legends, they were a family line still walking around with iron beds nine cubits long.

Og king of Bashan had a bedstead so massive scripture stops the story to record the dimensions.

The Rephaim, the Zamzummim, the Emim.

Tribes.

Plural.

Named.

These were not flood survivors. The flood killed them.

These came after.

The same way they came before.
