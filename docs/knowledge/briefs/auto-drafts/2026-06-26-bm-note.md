you didn't leave God.

you left a person. someone who carried a Bible and used it like a club. right words on Sunday, cold heart on Tuesday. you met religion and called it God. they were never the same thing.

here is what nobody tells the wounded. Jesus had a bad experience with religious people too.

the most religious men of His day knew the scriptures cold. they tithed the mint. they prayed loud on the corner. and they nailed God to a tree.

so when religion burns you, you are not far from Christ. you are standing exactly where He stood. mocked by the men who should have known better.

don't let the men who hurt you become the reason you bury the God who didn't. the Pharisee is not the standard. the cross is.

walk past the doormen. He is still inside.
