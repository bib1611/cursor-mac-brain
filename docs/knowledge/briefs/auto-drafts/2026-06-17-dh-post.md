# The Smartest Men in the Room Are the Most Deceived

Romans 1:22.

"Professing themselves to be wise, they became fools."

That verse is not about your uncle on Facebook.

It is about the men with the letters after their names.

The chairs. The podiums. The peer reviews. The blue check anchors. The seminary deans. The pulpit stars.

They are the ones the strong delusion is built for.

## The Pattern Paul Saw

He wrote it in chains.

He had watched the smartest men of Athens stand on Mars Hill and laugh at the resurrection. He had watched the Pharisees, the most credentialed religious men in the empire, deliver the Son of God to a Roman pole.

The educated men.

The literate men.

The men who could quote the law in two languages.

They missed Him.

The fishermen did not miss Him.

The thief on the cross did not miss Him.

The Samaritan woman at the well did not miss Him.

The smart men missed Him.

## What Credentials Do to a Mind

Every degree is a discipleship.

Every program you sit under shapes you. Every paper you publish you cannot un-publish. Every conference you keynote you have to keep keynoting. Every consensus you signed is a leash you are wearing.

You cannot say the firmament is real if your tenure depends on saying there is no firmament.

You cannot say the giants were real if your seminary salary depends on saying Genesis 6 is mythology.

You cannot say the King James Bible is preserved scripture if your publishing contract depends on the new translation coming out next year.

You cannot say the orbs are demonic if the federal grant requires you to call them extraterrestrial.

So you do not say it.

You stay employed.

You stay published.

You stay invited to the panel.

You stay deceived.

## The Inversion

The world tells you the man with no letters does not know.

The Bible tells you the man with no letters has nothing in the way.

A reader wrote me yesterday. He quoted Romans 1:22 and said it about the people in his own life who tire him. He said the smartest ones get worse, not better. He said it because his son writes truth and the world calls his son simple.

The son is not simple.

The world is simple. The world is one note. The world is reading the same press release in twelve different voices.

The son is reading the Book.

That is the only difference.

## What the Strong Delusion Actually Is

You think the delusion is a UFO landing.

It is not.

It is a man with a PhD on television telling you what your Bible says is metaphor.

It is a pastor with a microphone telling you the Greek says the opposite of what the King James says.

It is a senator who took the oath on a Bible he does not believe explaining why your faith is the threat to the republic.

It is a documentary that quotes nine sources and not the one Source.

It is the steady, sober, well-paid voice telling you to trust the men paid to lie.

2 Thessalonians 2:11.

"And for this cause God shall send them strong delusion, that they should believe a lie."

Read it again.

God sends it.

He hands them over to the wisdom they kept choosing over Him.

## Who Sees Through It

The farmer with a King James on his nightstand.

The widow with eight grandchildren and four chapters a day.

The garbage truck driver at twenty below thinking about Job.

The kid in juvenile detention reading a Gideon Bible somebody left in the cell.

The woman in the wheelchair who lost her son and stopped believing the smart men years ago.

The drunk who got saved reading Romans 1 and never went back.

They see.

The men with the chairs do not see.

The Lord taketh pleasure in them that fear him, in them that hope in his mercy. Psalm 147:11.

He does not take pleasure in the panel discussion.

## What This Means for You

Stop apologizing for not having the letters.

Stop apologizing for not having read the latest book they all read.

Stop apologizing for not knowing the consensus.

The consensus is the delusion.

You have a King James Bible on the table. You have a roof over your head and breath in your lungs and the same Spirit that raised Christ from the dead living in you. You have everything the apostles had.

You have what the men with the chairs do not have.

A free mind.

## The Last Thing

The credentialed class is going to roll something out this year.

It is going to be slick.

It is going to be sober.

It is going to be backed by men with letters you cannot pronounce, from institutions you cannot afford, citing studies you cannot read.

It is going to be a lie.

You are going to know it is a lie because you have a Book they refuse to read.

Romans 1:22. Professing themselves to be wise, they became fools.

Keep reading the Book.

You are not the simple one.

You are the only one left who can still see.
