# The Verse the Pentagon Cannot Answer

Genesis 1:7.

Read it slow.

"And God made the firmament, and divided the waters which were under the firmament from the waters which were above the firmament: and it was so."

The waters above.

That is the line they cannot explain.

## What They Showed You

A blue marble.

Black space outside. Vacuum. Cold. Nothing.

You were taught that at six.

You drew it in third grade with a crayon.

You watched the cartoons with the rocket leaving the atmosphere into empty dark.

That model has waters under the dome. It does not have waters above.

Your Bible has both.

One of those two stories is lying to you.

## What the Pentagon Admitted

Orbs.

Tic-tac craft. Transmedium objects. Things moving in and out of the water. Things moving through the sky at speeds that should rip them apart.

The hearings were public.

The footage was declassified.

The senators sat there nodding while the pilots described things their physics could not catalog.

Then everyone went home.

Nobody opened a Bible.

## Genesis 1:7 Was the Map

If there are waters above the firmament, the things coming out of those waters are not aliens.

They are old.

They were here before you. They were watched by Job. They were named by Moses. They were warned about by Peter.

The angels that kept not their first estate. The sons of God that came in unto the daughters of men. The same yesterday, today, and forever.

A reader wrote me last night. He said it plain. "It is a lot easier to figure out who they are once you understand that we live beneath a firmament separating our world from the waters above. Just like Genesis explains."

He is not crazy.

He is reading the Book.

## What Got Erased

Every old map showed a dome.

Every ancient culture had a firmament. Egypt. Babylon. Greece. India. China.

They were not all stupid.

They all had the same testimony.

Then a man in a wig with a telescope sold the West a different story and the church bowed without a fight.

Now you cannot say firmament in seminary without losing your job.

You cannot say waters above without losing your followers.

You cannot say Genesis 6 in the pulpit without somebody filing a complaint.

So nobody says it.

The orbs keep coming anyway.

## And Also Afterward

Genesis 6:4.

"There were giants in the earth in those days; and also after that, when the sons of God came in unto the daughters of men, and they bare children to them, the same became mighty men which were of old, men of renown."

And also after that.

A reader pointed at that line yesterday. He said most people skip right past it.

He is right.

That phrase wrecks the bedtime version of the Flood.

If the sons of God came in unto the daughters of men before the flood and also afterward, then the bloodline did not die in Noah's ark.

The Bible is telling you the offspring kept coming.

You can find them in Numbers 13. You can find them in 1 Samuel 17. You can find them on the maps the Pentagon cannot publish.

## The Strong Delusion

The world wants a clean answer.

A spaceship from Andromeda. A peaceful federation. A new gospel from a higher consciousness.

Anything but Genesis.

Anything but the firmament. Anything but the sons of God. Anything but the warning in 2 Thessalonians 2:11.

"And for this cause God shall send them strong delusion, that they should believe a lie."

The lie has a budget now.

The lie has hearings.

The lie has documentaries.

You will see it on a screen near you within the year.

## The Question

If they roll out the disclosure event, and the orbs land in a stadium, and the men in suits say "we are not alone," what will you do?

Will you watch with the world?

Or will you open Genesis?

The pilot does not have the verse.

The senator does not have the verse.

The astrophysicist does not have the verse.

You do.

## The Last Thing

The Pentagon admitted the orbs are real.

Your Bible named them first.

Genesis 1:7 is the verse they cannot answer because it ends the model they have been selling since you were six.

Waters above.

Sons of God.

Old enemies.

Same yesterday, today, and forever.

Keep reading the Book. The map was always there.
