# BM Substack Note — 2026-06-27

Elijah called fire down from heaven. Killed four hundred prophets of Baal. The biggest day of his ministry.

The very next morning he was under a tree, begging God to take his life.

I used to think that meant his faith broke. It didn't. His body ran out of fuel.

God's answer wasn't a sermon. It was bread and a nap. "Arise and eat; because the journey is too great for thee."

If you woke up empty today, you're in good company. Moses asked to die. So did Jonah. Faithfulness isn't the absence of the empty morning. It's showing up to it.

Get up. Eat. He's not done with you.
