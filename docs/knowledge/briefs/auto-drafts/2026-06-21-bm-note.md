# BM Note

You provide everything.

Bills paid. Roof solid. Freezer full.

And you sit at your own table, surrounded by your own children, and feel like you are watching them through glass.

I was that man.

The provider learns to function without feeling. It pays the mortgage. It also turns a father into a roof and a wallet and a ride to practice.

Present in the house. Gone from the home.

Revelation 3:17 has a word for the man who needs nothing. Wretched. Poor. Blind. Naked.

Then God does not walk away. He stands at the door and knocks.

The numb man cannot will himself warm. He just opens the door.

New post today, for every father reading this through glass.
