# BM Substack Note — 2026-06-17

There is a sentence sitting in your chest right now.

You have not said it out loud in ten years.

Maybe thirty.

You think the silence is keeping you safe.

It is the reason your sleep died.

It is the reason the prayer feels like talking to a ceiling.

It is the reason your wife says you are a million miles away in the same room.

Psalm 32:3. When I kept silence, my bones waxed old through my roaring all the day long.

Say the thing tonight.

To Him first. To one trusted man this week.

The bones will start to come back.
