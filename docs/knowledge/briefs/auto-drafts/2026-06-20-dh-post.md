# The Jesus They Cropped Out

You were handed a soft picture.

Pale robe. Gentle eyes. A lamb in his arms and not a mark on him.

That picture is not a lie. It is a crop.

They cut the frame down until only the comfort was left.

Then they sold you the corner and called it the whole man.

## Open the Last Book

John saw him. The same John who leaned on his chest at supper.

This time John fell down like a dead man.

His head and his hairs were white like wool, as white as snow; and his eyes were as a flame of fire. Revelation 1:14.

Eyes like fire. Not eyes that overlook. Eyes that read you to the floor.

His feet like unto fine brass, as if they burned in a furnace. His voice as the sound of many waters. Revelation 1:15.

Out of his mouth went a sharp twoedged sword. Revelation 1:16.

That is the Lamb.

He still has teeth.

## Why They Hid Him

A soft Jesus asks nothing of you.

He nods. He affirms. He waits in the corner of the frame for the times you feel low.

The cropped Jesus is safe to keep on a shelf.

The whole Jesus walks in among the candlesticks and tells the church he loves what he hates about it. Read the letters in Revelation 2 and 3. He is not gentle with rot.

I have, somewhat against thee. Revelation 2:4.

The flattened one never says that. He cannot. They cut his mouth out of the picture.

## You Asked for More of Him

A reader wrote this week. Said the resurrection drew her in.

Said she wants more studies on Jesus. The real ones.

So here is the real one.

He is meek and he is fire. He is the lamb slain and the lion of Judah. He wept at a grave and he will judge the living and the dead.

And his name is called The Word of God. Revelation 19:13.

Out of his mouth goeth a sharp sword. Revelation 19:15.

Same sword. Same mouth. The one they cropped.

## The Turn

Stop worshipping the corner of the frame.

The comfort is real. But it is not the whole man, and a half-Jesus will not hold you when the dark comes for everything.

Open the last book. Meet the one with eyes of fire.

Fall down like John did.

Then get up. He will put his right hand on you and say what he said to John.

Fear not. Revelation 1:17.

That is the Jesus they cropped out.

That is the one worth following.
