# BM Substack Note — 2026-06-20

The serpent did not start with a lie.

He started with a question.

Yea, hath God said?

He did not rip the page out of her hand. He just made her squint at it.

That is still the whole game. He does not need you to burn the Book. He only needs you to mistrust one line of it.

One verse. One whisper. And the muzzle is on.

But read Psalm 12. He did not just speak the word. He swore to keep it.

So when the dark asks if God really said it, do not answer the dark.

Answer it with the verse. Out loud. Tonight.
