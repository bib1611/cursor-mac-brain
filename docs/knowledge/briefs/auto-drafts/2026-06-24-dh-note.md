# DH Note — 2026-06-24

Something lived in my house before I was born.

I never saw it. I felt it.

It moved through the men in my family like a draft through old wood. Every room it touched got colder.

A reader told me his mother was beaten with extension cords. He said violence and drink were the most reliable things in the house. Reliable. The terror showed up on schedule.

That is what the old book means by the iniquity of the fathers, visited to the third and fourth generation.

The bent travels. The appetite. The rage you didn't earn.

You think you chose it. You didn't. It was waiting in the walls when they brought you home.

Some things are not patterns. Some things are spirits that learned your family's address.

You can medicate a pattern.

You cannot medicate a thing that wants your children.

But it answers to one Name. The blood of Jesus Christ cleanseth from all sin.

That is not a poem. That is an eviction notice.

The draft can stop with you.
