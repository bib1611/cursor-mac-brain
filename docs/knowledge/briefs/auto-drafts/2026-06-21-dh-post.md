# They Made the Angels Small

They put wings on a baby and called it heaven.

Pink cheeks. A little harp. A chubby cherub floating over a greeting card.

That is not what walked out of the pages.

When a real one showed up, grown men hit the floor.

## What Actually Arrived

Daniel was a prophet. A man who stared down lions and kings.

One angel came near and he came apart.

There remained no strength in me: for my comeliness was turned in me into corruption, and I retained no strength. Daniel 10:8.

His face went grey. His knees gave out. The strongest man in Babylon dropped like a corpse.

The soldiers at the tomb were trained Romans.

And the keepers did shake, and became as dead men. Matthew 28:4.

Read that again. The guards played dead because they thought death had finally come for them.

This is the thing they shrank down and stuck on a Christmas ornament.

## Why Every One Of Them Says The Same Three Words

Count how many times an angel opens with Fear not.

Zacharias in the temple. Mary. The shepherds in the field. Every single time.

Fear not.

You do not say that to a man looking at a cute baby with wings.

You say it to a man who is certain he is about to be unmade.

The first sound a real angel makes is the sound that calms a heart about to stop.

## What The Shrinking Costs You

A small angel guards nothing.

A small angel cannot stand at the edge of your dark room with a flaming sword. He cannot smite an army in the night. He cannot roll back a stone the size of a wall and sit on it like a chair.

They made the messenger small so the message would feel small.

A tame heaven. A safe God. A faith that fits on a shelf next to the candles.

But the host that surrounds Elisha is still fire and horses on the mountain.

And the LORD opened the eyes of the young man; and he saw: and, behold, the mountain was full of horses and chariots of fire round about Elisha. 2 Kings 6:17.

They were always there.

You just got handed a cartoon and told to stop looking.

## Open Your Eyes

The heaven they sell you is decoration.

The one in the Book will drop you to your knees.

Ask God to show you the real thing. Then hold on.

Because once you have seen the size of what stands behind you, you stop being afraid of what stands in front of you.
