# DH Substack Note — 2026-06-22

They handed you a bathtub and called it the end of the world.

Two giraffes. A smiling lion. A boat the size of a backyard shed.

Ask the cubit question and the cartoon dies.

Shrink the ark and you shrink the flood. Shrink the flood and you shrink the judgment. Shrink the judgment and you shrink the God who sent it.

That is the whole game.

A small flood is a fairy tale. It asks nothing of you.

A flood that drowned a world is a warning.

They taught you to picture a toy so you would never picture the door.

The next one has your generation's name on it.
