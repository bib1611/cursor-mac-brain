# The Scoffer Was Foretold. He Calls Himself Wise.

He stands in the comment section like a small king.

Arms folded. Brow heavy. Mouth full of clever.

He asks where the promise is.

He thinks the question is original. It is two thousand years old.

He thinks he invented doubt. He inherited it.

"Knowing this first, that there shall come in the last days scoffers, walking after their own lusts, and saying, Where is the promise of his coming?" — 2 Peter 3:3–4

You were told he was coming.

You were not told he would look like this. Soft hands. A nice avatar. A podcast.

But he was coming.

---

The scoffer is not loud anymore. He is reasonable.

He says he just has questions. He says he just wants evidence.

He does not want evidence. He wants permission.

Permission to keep what he loves. Permission to keep the lust. Permission to keep the throne.

Read the verse again. "Walking after their own lusts." That is the engine. The mouth is just the exhaust.

The argument was never the argument.

---

He calls himself a freethinker.

He thinks freedom is the absence of authority. He has never met a chain that quiet.

You watch a man parade his unbelief like a medal. He does not know it is a leash.

A man who cannot kneel is not standing. He is propped up by his own pride and one strong wind from collapse.

The Lord will be that wind.

---

He says, "All things continue as they were from the beginning of the creation." Verse four. Word for word.

He says it on the morning the sky cracks.

Peter watched this play out in his mind seventeen hundred years before it streamed on a six-inch screen.

The scoffer does not see how predictable he is.

That is the joke. The Bible spoiled his role two millennia early. He still thinks he is being original.

---

You do not have to argue with him.

You were not commanded to win the comment war.

You were commanded to be ready.

"Be ready always to give an answer to every man that asketh you a reason of the hope that is in you with meekness and fear." — 1 Peter 3:15

Note the word. Meekness. Not contempt.

He is not the enemy. He is the casualty.

The enemy used him to bait you out of position. Do not take the bait.

---

There is one answer he cannot scoff at.

Not a verse. Not a meme. Not a syllogism.

A man whose life does not match the world. A wife unbroken under pressure. A son who looks like his father in stillness, not just in face.

A house with a Bible open at the kitchen table and no apology for it.

That is the apologetic the scoffer cannot break.

He can argue your words. He cannot argue your hours.

---

He says the date is a lie. He says the warning is a meme. He says you are afraid of nothing.

He is the proof.

The prophecy did not say signs first, then scoffers. It said scoffers first.

He is the bell.

When you see him ringing, you do not panic. You finish what you were doing.

You teach the boy. You bury the dead. You feed the hungry. You read the Book.

The Lord has not changed His mind about coming.

He is just letting one more scoffer fill out his role.

---

*Pin this somewhere he can find it. Do not tag him. He will arrive on his own.*
