# God Doesn't Need a Clean Vessel

A reader wrote me yesterday.

His grandfather's friend got saved by a drunk in a bar mocking a traveling evangelist.

The drunk was making fun.

Reading the gospel in a slurred voice. Imitating the preacher. Playing it for laughs.

A man at the next stool heard it. Really heard it. The words went past the joke and into his chest. He left the bar a different man.

The drunk was the messenger.

The drunk did not know.

## The Lie They Sell You

The lie is that God needs your seminary. Your Greek. Your committee. Your peer review.

The lie is that the Word needs handlers. Curators. Men in cardigans with footnotes.

The lie is that the message only works if the vessel is washed.

But God spoke through a donkey.

Numbers 22. Balaam's ass. The animal saw what the prophet did not. Opened her mouth and rebuked a man who was selling out for money. The donkey was more spiritual than the seer.

That is on purpose.

## The Father of Lies Has a PhD

The men who handle the text most carefully are often the ones who trust it least.

They will tell you Mark 16 is a late addition. That John 7:53 is a fairy tale. That 1 John 5:7 is a Latin gloss that crept in.

They strip the sword and call it scholarship.

Westcott held seances. Hort doubted the resurrection. They are the men who gave the modern church its critical text. The fingerprints are still on the paper.

Meanwhile a drunk in a bar gets a man saved by mocking the King James.

The Word does not need the scholar. The scholar needs the Word.

## What the Bible Says About Vessels

God hath chosen the foolish things of the world to confound the wise. 1 Corinthians 1:27.

The foolish.

The base.

Things which are despised hath God chosen, yea, and things which are not, to bring to nought things that are.

That is the kingdom blueprint.

He picks the man no one would pick. He picks the fisherman over the scribe. He picks the prostitute over the priest. He picks the murderer with a stutter to confront Pharaoh.

He picks you.

Not because you are clean.

Because He is.

## The Drunk Was Not the Hero

Hear me. I am not glorifying the drunk.

I am saying the Word did not lose its power when it crossed his lips.

It is not the holiness of the vessel that activates the verse. It is the holiness of the verse that exposes the vessel.

A clean man can read it dead.

A drunk man can read it and the Holy Ghost gets to work without permission.

That should terrify you and free you in the same breath.

It means the pulpit is not protected from death.

It means the bar is not exempt from life.

## The JW With the New World Translation

Same reader said his dad once preached the gospel to a Jehovah's Witness using the man's own New World Translation.

Sat down.

Opened the book that was made to hide Jesus.

And used it to find Him.

That is what the Word does. It pierces. Even through corrupted ink. Even through bad translation. Even through bad doctrine. The arrow finds the seam.

Now I am KJV all the way. I do not read the NWT. I do not preach from the NWT.

But the Word in the King James was so present in that father that it cut through a counterfeit Bible to reach the man holding it.

The vessel did not stop it.

The Word did not stop.

## What This Means for You

Stop waiting until you are clean to speak.

You will never be clean.

Speak anyway.

The neighbor over the fence. The cousin on the phone. The waitress refilling your coffee at 4 AM.

You do not have to perform. You do not have to memorize the systematics. You do not have to wait for permission from a man with a degree.

You have a Bible. You have a tongue. You have a story.

That is more than the apostle Paul had when he was still Saul.

## The Quiet Truth

The Word of God is alive.

Hebrews 4:12.

Quick. Powerful. Sharper than any twoedged sword. Piercing even to the dividing asunder of soul and spirit.

It does not need you to be sharp.

It is the sword.

You are the hand.

Sometimes the hand is shaking. Sometimes the hand is drunk. Sometimes the hand belongs to a man who does not even believe what he is reading.

The sword still cuts.

That is the whole story.

That is why the gates of hell cannot prevail.
