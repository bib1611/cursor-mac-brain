# DH Substack Note — 2026-06-19

The first weapon ever used against a human being was a question.

The first wound ever inflicted on the human race was a doubt.

"Yea, hath God said."

Four words. Six thousand years. Every kingdom of man built on top of them.

You have heard it in the footnotes of your Bible.

"The earliest manuscripts do not contain this verse." Yea, hath God said.

You have heard it from the pulpit.

"What this really means in the original." Yea, hath God said.

You have heard it in your own chest at 2 AM.

"Did God really mean it."

The serpent does not need to enter the room anymore. He left the sentence in the wallpaper.

Jesus answered it three times in the wilderness with three words.

"It is written."

That is the sword. Pick up a Bible the serpent did not get to edit.
