# BM Note — 2026-06-18

You have been beating the wrong animal.

For years.

The wife who will not let you walk back into the sin.

The friend who keeps bringing up the verse you do not want to hear.

The closed door at the company.

The illness that put you in the chair where you finally opened the Book.

You called it the obstacle.

Balaam called the donkey the obstacle.

The donkey was the only thing standing between him and a sword he could not see.

Numbers 22 is the chapter most men need this week and will not read.

Repent to the donkey tonight.

Whoever she is.
