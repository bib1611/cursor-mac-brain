# DH Substack Note — 2026-06-20

You were handed a soft picture of Jesus.

Pale robe. Gentle eyes. Not a mark on him.

That picture is not a lie. It is a crop. They cut the frame down until only the comfort was left.

Open the last book. The John who leaned on his chest at supper fell down like a dead man.

His eyes were as a flame of fire. Out of his mouth went a sharp twoedged sword.

That is the Lamb. He still has teeth.

A soft Jesus asks nothing of you. The whole Jesus walks in among the candlesticks and tells the church the truth about itself.

Stop worshipping the corner of the frame. Meet the one with eyes of fire.
