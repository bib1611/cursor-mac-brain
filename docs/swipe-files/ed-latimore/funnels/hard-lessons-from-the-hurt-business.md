## You don’t have to take the punches I did. But you can learn from every one of them.

From a violent childhood and street fights to addiction and the relentless grind of the boxing ring, *Hard Lessons from the Hurt Business* is my raw, unfiltered story of how I turned pain into purpose and hardship into personal power.

This isn’t just a memoir. **It’s a survival manual** **for anyone battling chaos** —whether it’s poverty, addiction, toxic relationships, or self-doubt. With the bruised knuckles of a fighter and the sharp mind of a physics graduate, I lay out how I escaped a destructive path, found discipline through boxing, and rewired my mind to reroute my life to win on my own terms.

If you’ve ever felt stuck, underestimated, or one bad break away from rock bottom, this book is the corner coach you didn’t know you needed. Honest, gripping, and at times hilarious, *Hard Lessons From The Hurt Business* will remind you that your past doesn’t define your future—but only if you use them now to build a better future.
