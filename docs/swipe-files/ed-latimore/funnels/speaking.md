## Highlights from Ed’s most requested speech

Through gripping personal stories—from street fights to the boxing ring, from addiction to sobriety—Ed equips audiences with tools to face their own battles, make decisive changes, and emerge stronger than ever.

*Hard Lessons From The Hurt Business: Four Principles To Make Everlasting Change* delivers the following key takeaways:

1

### Control what you can control

Ed shows you how to stop wasting energy on things outside your influence and focus on what you can change right now. This shift creates momentum and builds confidence.

* Recognize the difference between controllable and uncontrollable factors
* Redirect wasted effort into actions that create immediate progress
* Apply a physics-based framework for maximizing impact with minimal wasted energy

2

### Face the truth head-on

Lasting change begins with brutal honesty. Ed teaches how to confront the real problem instead of hiding behind excuses or distractions.

* Learn the cost of ignoring reality—and how to pay less by acting early
* Break the cycle of denial and self-sabotage
* Use candid self-assessment to unlock growth and resilience

3

### Bet on yourself

Ed’s story proves that risk is the price of reward. By investing in yourself—even when resources are scarce—you can transform obstacles into opportunities.

* Discover how to make bold moves that accelerate growth
* Build confidence by taking calculated risks on your potential
* Shift from being a passive player in someone else’s game to creating your own winning odds

4

### Redefine your identity

Discipline and motivation fade—but identity endures. Ed shows why the fastest way to lasting change is to change who you believe you are.

* Replace self-limiting labels with empowering identities
* Learn why identity shifts outlast willpower and resolutions
* Adopt the mindset of a fighter: someone who acts from strength, not desperation

5

### *The Fight Plan*

Audiences leave with a clear, actionable framework:

* Control what you can
* Tell the truth
* Bet on yourself
* Change your identity
