October 1st is the day

Dakota Robertson

Aug 22, 2024

For the past few months I’ve been working on my new community offer, Full Stack Creator.

And to be honest?

I’ve been struggling…

You see, I have a tendency to be a bit of a perfectionist. 

Probably because I grew up as a chubby kid and wanted others to like me lol.

So when it came to building out this new community offer, I wanted to make it amazing and leave nothing out.

But that also meant I was overthinking everything and taking too long.

And I’ve realized I just need to get my ass in gear and set a date for it or it’ll never get done.

So, the launch date for Full Stack Creator is October 1st.

Here’s some quick info about the offer:

WTF Is a Full Stack Creator?

A Full Stack Creator is someone who:

Learns a skill

Builds a social media audience

Stacks complementary skills as they become proficient at one

For example:

Web design + Copywriting

Ghostwriting + Email marketing

Video editing + Content strategy

This allows you to:

Differentiate yourself from competition

Provide more value to clients

Puts you in a category of 1

You can then charge higher prices as demand increases for your services. 

Ultimately, a Full Stack Creator moves away from chasing clients for peanuts to attracting high-ticket clients.

Examples of Full Stack Creators:

Dan Koe

Pat Flynn

Tim Ferriss

Iman Gadzhi

Alex Hormozi

What’s included in the offer?

I’ll give you more details in the coming weeks, but to put it simply:

Calls: live workshops with me going over lessons

Course: an extensive and easy to follow course with plug n’ play frameworks for monetizing your personal brand.

Community: a membership of like-minded people on the same journey as you.

Who Is Full Stack Creator For?

Freelancers

Working professionals

Pineapple pizza lovers

Coaches & consultants

Who Is Full Stack Creator NOT For?

If you don’t have an actionable online skill that helps people get a quantifiable outcome, this is not for you.

What Problems Does Full Stack Creator Solve?
Time

Nobody wants to spend 16 hours a learning how to create content or spamming messages in hopes of getting clients.

I’ve made plug and play systems for everything so you can stop wasting time doing shit that doesn’t work.

Information overload and focu

1 guru says do X, another tells you to do Y.

I’ve laid out everything you need to know about growing and monetizing your personal brand into an easy to follow course.

Wasting hours doing outreach

Sending mass amounts of DMs and emails to get clients is time-consuming, takes a lot of effort, and is hard to scale.

It leaves you stuck on the never-ending hamster wheel of doing countless hours of outreach every week just to get 1 client.

Converting clients

Even if you get the attention of prospects, it’s hard to convert them to clients if they don’t know, like, or trust you. 

This can lead to unreliable income and being stuck managing a big team that’s time-consuming and stressful.

By becoming a Full Stack Creator you’ll put yourself in a category of one and be able to charge premium prices for your services.

Join The Waitlist For Full Stack Creator

I’ll reveal more details on everything in the coming weeks, but if you want to get on the waitlist click here.

The first people to join will get the lowest price possible.

Much love.

Your Canadian friend,

Dakota “Full Stack Robertson
