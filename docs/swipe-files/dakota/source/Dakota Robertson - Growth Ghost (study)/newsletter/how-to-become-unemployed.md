How to become unemployed
Dakota Robertson

Jan 13, 2024

One of the best feelings is when I see people become unemployed.

It’s great.

And no, not because I’m some sadistic sicko…

I love it because seeing members from my program quit their job to go all in on ghostwriting is wholesome.

I know what it’s like to work for people you don’t respect.

I know what it’s like to work endless hours and barely scrape by.

I know what it’s like to work a grueling job you have no interest in.

It’s draining.

So when I get to help people transition from that…

To a life where they:

Live how they want

Work on what they want

Wake up when they want

It’s fulfilling for me.

So…

If you want my help doing the same, click here.

I’ve opened up applications for my cohort.

But only until January 21st (8 more days), so I’d act now if you’re interested.

Your Canadian friend,

Dakota “Unemployed” Robertson

P.S.

I also help people become college dropouts ;)
