It's over.
Dakota Robertson

Oct 10, 2024

It’s been a long week, ladies and gents, but it’s finally over.

I launched Full Stack Creator (as I’m sure you know from me promoting it non-stop lol).

And to be honest, it was a lot on me.

I was overseeing emails, content, automations, building out the community, writing the VSL/copy, helping create the landing page.

Leading up to launch I was up for 37+ hours straight, abusing nicotine, caffeine, and Modafinil.

At one point I literally worked for 18 hours straight.

I almost said “screw it all” before the launch and canceled it because I was so overwhelmed from the stress of doing everything.

And that’s what I wanted to briefly touch on in this email.

Despite what you might think from seeing my impeccable jawline and charming personality…

I don’t have everything figured out.

Hell, ever since the start I’ve been forced to push my limits and do more than I thought possible.

With sales…

With content…

With marketing..

But every time, I forced myself to because I gave myself “Tactical Anxiety.”

Either by dropping outta college, investing in a coaching program, or publicly announcing a launch date for an offer.

I put myself in a position where I didn’t have any other option but to do the damn thing.

And that’s what I’ve noticed with many of my most successful friends.

They don’t have it figured out, but they still do it anyway. 

Because they know that if they sit on the sidelines “thinking,” it won’t get them closer to their goals.

You just gotta jump in and have faith that you’ll figure it out as you go.

I’m still burnt out af from this week, so nothing too crazy in depth for this email. 

Just wanted to share that with ya.

And thank you to everyone who supported the launch or bought the membership.

Truly appreciate you guys and gals. It means a lot.

(to everyone who didn’t, I hope you spontaneously combust).

Jk, I still love you.

Your Canadian friend,

Dakota “I Need Sleep” Robertson
