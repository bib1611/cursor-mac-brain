One that’s helped my clients scale to:

$3K/months.

All without having a degree, past business experience, or being an AI nerd. 

Here's how...

The Social Media Gold Rush

With the rise of the creator economy, people like:

Gary V

Dan Koe

Iman Gadzhi 

Alex Hormozi

Have all made millions from growing their personal brand on social media.

And there are 1000s of entrepreneurs seeing them and thinking to themselves:

“I should probably post content.”

But here’s the thing…

They don’t.

Because they don’t have the:

1) Time.

2) The know-how. 

And let’s be real… most of them write like they learned English from watching Jersey Shore reruns.

That's where AI ghostwriters come in...

AI ghostwriters make entrepreneurs more money by writing their content.

For example:

An entrepreneur sells a $5,000 offer. 

You use AI to create the content. 

You get them 500 followers.

If 1% convert? 

Client makes $25,000. 

If you charge $3K-$5K/month? 

Easy sell.

How To Start AI Ghostwriting (Without Being a Writing Expert)
Step 1) Find Clients

Use LinkedIn Sales Navigator to find potential clients:

→ CEOs + Founders 

→ AI + Tech Startups 

→ Coaches + Consultants

Anyone selling an online product/service.

Step 2) Reach Out

Look for people with low engagement or inconsistent posting.

Reach out and offer a free trial post.

Your goal?

Show authority and book a call to pitch your AI ghostwriting service.

Step 3) Pitch Your Ghostwriting Service

Entrepreneurs don't buy ghostwriting.

They buy ROI on their time + money.

For example:

“I’ll get you ___ leads from social media and save you 10+ hours a week.”

Sell the desired outcome, not ghostwriting.

Step 4) Use AI To Ghostwrite

Book 1 call a month with your client.

Ask them questions (their life + expertise).

Use Fathom AI to transcribe the call.

Upload the transcription to ChatGPT.

Use AI prompts to write the content.

The clients “write” the content for you on the interview.

Your job?

Ask them good questions.

Copy/paste the right AI prompts to write the content.

Edit the posts so it’s optimized for social media.

Step 5) Scale (Without Burning Out)

Here’s a general timeline for scaling with AI ghostwriting:

Months 1-3 

↳ Land 1-2 clients.

↳ Create systems.

Months 4-6 

↳ Hire a VA for low ROI tasks. 

↳ Take on 2-3 more clients. 

↳ Hire 1-2 writers.

Months 7-12 

↳ Train a writer to manage your clients. 

↳ Focus on landing more clients.

How To Make More Dolla Dolla Billz

Clients need new content every month.

Each new client = More $ per month.

You can also upsell clients and ghostwrite their emails or other social media.

That’s how you turn $3K/month clients into $10K/month clients.

So yeah… AI ghostwriting is basically legal money laundering for people with WiFi.

You don’t need to be a genius.

You don’t need a fancy degree.

You just need to not be a complete potato.

And if you are a potato? That’s okay too.
