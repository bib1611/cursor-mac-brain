How to make $10K/month in the next 6 months

Your roadmap to making money

Dakota Robertson

Jun 29, 2023

I’ve noticed the thing holding most people back from making money online isn’t their ability.

It’s their beliefs.

When I started on Twitter 3 years ago, I heard of people making $10,000/month.

It blew my mind.

But after surrounding myself around those people, it became normalized.

And because it became normalized, I believed I could get there too.

It wasn’t all sunshine and rainbows, but with the help of my mentor, I hit it within 28 days of launching my ghostwriting business.

That same mentor, was also the first person I saw hit $50,000/month.

My brain had a malfunction. I could barely process how much that was.

Again, a new set of beliefs were shattered.

But by surrounding myself with that caliber of people — like $10K/month — it became normalized again.

And this is where I think most people screw up...

They see or hear of someone hitting those numbers and immediately label it as a scam or lies.

Mind you, there are a ton out there, so please do your due diligence.

But teachers, family, friends, and society have conditioned us to think small.

You’re told to “be realistic.”

And because of this, anything that goes against the narrative is rejected.

Brainwash yourself

The key to overcoming your programming is to brainwash yourself.

Cut out anyone that laughs at your goals.

Surround yourself with delusionally optimistic friends.

Read books from those who’ve accomplished what you want in life.

Curate your social media feed to show the accounts educating instead of entertaining.

Become ruthless with the energy you surround yourself with.

Do this for a few weeks and you’ll notice something interesting happen…

Goals that used to seem impossible will now seem like a matter of time.

$10,000/month.

$100,000/month.

$1,000,000/month.

There are legit people providing real value hitting those numbers.

And the funny thing is...

They’re not wildly gifted or big brain IQ geniuses.

They’re like you and me.

They just believed it was possible.

And because of that belief, they looked for ways to make it a reality.

It can be hard to overcome the conditioning we’ve been told throughout our lives growing up, but it with the right skill set and consistency, you can do it.

If you’re starting out with making money online, I recommend doing this:


1. Pick a skill you have an interest in.

I recommend one that you can either make other people more money, save them time, or improve their health.

• Closer

• Copywriter

• Ghostwriter

• Fitness coach

Again, it’s important you pick one you’re interested in. If it’s not aligned with what you enjoy, it’ll feel like work and you’ll never become exceptional. This can take time and some experimentation. Be patient.



2. Educate yourself.

Read the book, “Expert Secrets” by Brunson. 

This will give you a fantastic roadmap to follow for your journey. Then, consume free content from those in your field, or, if you can afford it, pay for mentorship. 

You’ll avoid all the mistakes they made and accomplish your goals 10x faster and save yourself costly mistakes.



3. Pick 1 social media platform and teach what you’re learning.

Talk about your experience and share your wins/failures. You don’t need to be an expert.

You just need to teach those a few steps behind you.



4. Package your skills together.

Read the book, “$100M Offers” by Hormozi.

Package the skill you’re learning into an offer using his framework.

Since you’re building an audience, you’ll have at least a few people who know, like, and trust you.

Leverage this to turn them into paying clients or work for free for a testimonial.

Working with your first client will teach you what challenges you’ll need to solve. Become relentless with figuring it out. Once you do, create repeatable systems and frameworks.



5. Become obsessed with getting results for your clients.

As you get more experience, you’ll become better (crazy how that works). As you become better, you can use it as social proof.

Leverage this social proof to land more clients, deliver more value, and make more money.


Do this for a year and I guarantee you’ll be living a completely new life.

So, I hope this gives you a clear roadmap to execute on, my friend.

With that being said, have a kickass day. Much love.

Your Canadian friend,
