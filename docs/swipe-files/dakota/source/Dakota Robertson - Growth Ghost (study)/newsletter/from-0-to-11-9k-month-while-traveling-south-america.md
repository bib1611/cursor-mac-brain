The "Free Work Flywheel"

Here's the 3 steps we took to scale him from $0 to $11.9K/month:

1) Escape The “Fat Fitness Coach Trap”

You wouldn’t hire a fat fitness coach to get you fit…

So why would you hire a ghostwriter with no followers to grow your social media?

This is the #1 reason ghostwriters don’t land clients.

And why the 1st part of my program didn’t even talk about ghostwriting…

Instead, I taught Mike:

What strategy to use for social media.

How to brand himself to stand out.

How to create engaging content.

This helped Mike grow his following.

But more importantly:

Gave him credibility as a ghostwriter.

2) Find Your Guinea Pig

Once Mike understood how to grow on social media, he just needed 1 person to give him a chance to get results.

So, Mike took what he learned from my program and…

Offered free content to a friend.

Helped him go from 34 to 301 followers in 48 hours.

Screenshotted the results and got a testimonial.

3) Turning Content Into Clients

Once Mike had results, he posted it on Twitter.

Within 15 minutes? He got a DM about his service.

The next day? $3,000 client closed.

2 weeks later: another client

3 weeks later: another client

1 week later: another client

1 week after that: another client

Before he knew it, Mike went from $0 to $11,900.
