A Million-Dollar Brand On a Ramen Noodle Budget?
Dakota Robertson

Sep 25, 2024

The first big hurdle in my creator journey was learning how to grow my following.

But a bigger problem quickly arose:

I didn’t know how to monetize.

So after about a year on Twitter, my followers were growing but my bank account was still shrinking.

I was surviving on Ramen noodles (and going months without pineapple pizza).

That’s when I knew something had to change.

So I made a financial decision some would call crazy:

With only $8,000 left in my bank account, I invested $6000 to be coached by Dan Koe.

(This is what I call ‘tactical anxiety’, which can be created by putting a limit on time…or money…or both).

And damn did it ever work. Within 28 days of launching my ghostwriting offer I was at $11,000/month.

Within 8 months of launching my offer I hit $50,000/month.

Now let me ask you…

Which is crazier:

Going $100K into debt for a college degree that isn’t even guaranteed to get you a job?

Or dropping $6000 to build a $50,000/month business?

And here’s the thing:

Even though it was scary investing that much money at one time…and not being 100% certain it was going to work…

It wasn’t nearly as scary as continuing to work dead-end jobs I hated while completely squandering my potential.

That’s something I just couldn’t live with.

And that’s why I’m grateful I chose a different path.

Fast forward 3 years, and I’ve got a plug-and-play system that has grown my brand to 500K+ followers on social media and helped me hit $100K+ months.

I’m going to reveal how you can do the same in my new community, Full Stack Creator, launching on October 1st.
