Aside from asking for feet pics, this is one of the most common questions I get asked. 

So instead of writing a super long email you probably won’t read… 

I thought I’d show you 4 messages and let them do most of the talking…

And then there’s my man, Vinchenso. Even with a family and managing real estate, he was able to make his first $1,000 online. Not by building his own business (yet), but by writing for someone else’s agency. 

Now, am I saying you’re going to do the same? 

YES, YOU’LL BE MAKING $690,420/MONTH BY TOMORROW OMGGGGGGG!!!!!

Kidding.

All these people worked extremely hard and put in consistent effort over a long time.

And anyone promising easy money like this overnight is lying to you.

But my point is that it is possible.

ALL these people had a lot of stuff going on in their lives.

Some have kids

Some had jobs

Some had a bunch of boring-ass lectures to go to

But with the right system, frameworks, and methodology to follow, they were able to execute.

They didn’t need to waste time wondering what to do because I mapped it all out for them.

Did they have to sacrifice time with friends and partying to sit in and write content or work on the biz?

Sure.

But that’s the price they were willing to pay to build a life on their terms.

So, am I saying you need to “hustle” for 18 hours a day?

Nah, you just set aside 1-3 hours a day.

But only if you have the right roadmap and system to execute on.
